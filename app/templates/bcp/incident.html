Incident Management Guide

Checklist: Track completion of immediate response tasks. Check off items as they're completed to monitor progress.

Contacts: Access key personnel and emergency contacts. Use this tab to quickly reach decision-makers and specialists during the incident.

Event Log: Document significant events during the incident. Record:

  • Decision points and who made them
  • Status updates and situation changes
  • Communications sent (internal and external)
  • Resource deployments or activations
  • Problems encountered and resolutions

Tip: Log events in real-time with your initials. This creates an audit trail for post-incident review.
