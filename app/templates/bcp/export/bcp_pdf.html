Staff Wellbeing Support

During and after a business disruption, staff wellbeing is a priority. The following resources are available:

  • Employee Assistance Program (EAP): Confidential counseling services
  • Beyond Blue: 1300 22 4636 - 24/7 support for anxiety and depression
  • Lifeline: 13 11 14 - 24-hour crisis support
  • 1800RESPECT: 1800 737 732 - Family violence counselling service

Managers should regularly check in with team members and facilitate access to support services as needed.
